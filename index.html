About Mount Rainier


Mountain Rainier is a large active stratovolcano in the Cascade Range of the Pacific Northwest, Located in Mountain Rainier National Park about 59 miles south-southeast of Seattle. Due to its high probability of an eruption in the near future, Mount Rainier is considered one of the most dangerous volcanoes in the world, and it is on the Decade Volcano list.
